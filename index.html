High Risk Interaction

Amoxicillin and Warfarin

Amoxicillin may increase the anticoagulant activities of Warfarin. This interaction may increase the risk of bleeding complications.

Recommendation: Monitor for signs of bleeding and adjust warfarin dosage as needed. Consider alternative antibiotics if possible.

About Med Pi

Med Pi is a comprehensive drug information platform designed to provide accurate, up-to-date medication information to both healthcare professionals and the general public.

Our mission is to improve medication safety and health outcomes by making reliable drug information accessible to everyone.

Founded in 2023 by a team of pharmacists and software engineers, Med Pi combines medical expertise with cutting-edge technology to deliver the most comprehensive drug database available.

All our information is reviewed by licensed pharmacists and updated regularly to reflect the latest FDA approvals, safety alerts, and clinical research.
